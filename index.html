How It Works

Being in the retail industry for almost a decade, We know how complicated it is to handle your sales process, manage your sales force and rely on the retail data to create successful strategy with the existing tools and methods. Depending on the size of the distribution company, the profit loss due to a lack of data can be counted up to six figures. We are here to change that.

Shelfer provides accurate, complete retail data in the shortest time than ever to improve your team efficiency, boost your sales and cut your profit losses. All in one app.

Why Shelfer

How many times have you lacked reliable data from retailers? Retail industry is dynamic and you need to receive the updates on the market situation as fast as possible.


Shelfer gives you an overview of the market, EDI (electronic data interchange) and helps you to directly instruct your sales force. Now you can control a complete process of sales data flow from retailer to the top management and the sales force to turn your monthly plans into profits in a blink of an eye.

Features

Shelf space allocation tracking

Adequate shelf space allocation can significantly improve your your sales and profit. Track your shelf space allocation in Shelfer and to make sure your goods are always on the right place.

EDI analysis

EDI saves your costs, accelerates your business, improves your efficiency and sales strategy. Get the most benefits from EDI by using Shelfer EDI analysis.

Assortment Data

Go paperless with your team. Your sales force will have an overview of assortment in each store in just a few clicks.

Client category management

In Shelfer,each store is labeled by the category management system, helping your sales representative to easily supervise the allocation of your goods in each category.


Sales force management

Effectively organize your sales force and promptly provide them the retail data for the maximum results.


Many more

Get the location of each store, plan your routes, place your orders, track the sales history and discover more helpful tools on Shelfer app.
